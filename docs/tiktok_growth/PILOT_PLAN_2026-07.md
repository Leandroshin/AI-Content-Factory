# TikTok audience growth pilot

Updated on 2026-07-11.

## Objective

Make `achadosbaratosbr2` an active, original and policy-compliant account while
building evidence for a future TikTok Shop Creator application. No follower
purchase, engagement exchange, copied video or automated person-like posting.

## Operating flow

Trend evidence -> scored shortlist -> Shin approval -> ContentBrief -> Script
-> Audio -> Image -> editorial beat map -> HyperFrames composition ->
EditorialQualityValidator -> separate 9:16 review -> final policy review ->
manual TikTok upload -> metrics after 24 hours and 7 days.

`core/content_factory/audience_growth.py` implements the evidence, scoring and
approval bridge. It intentionally reuses the existing production departments.

## Cadence

- Week 1: five videos, one per day.
- Weeks 2-4: five to seven videos per week, adjusted from retention data.
- Do not publish several nearly identical videos on the same day.
- Record views, average watch time, completion rate, shares, saves, comments
  and followers gained per video.

## Initial pillars

1. Gaming and GTA VI: official facts, details, comparisons and countdown.
2. Technology and AI: useful tools, real limitations and practical workflows.
3. Smart shopping: how to judge a deal, seller, image and price evidence.
4. Build in public: honest progress of building an AI content factory from
   zero, without income promises.

The account is not permanently locked to one niche. The planner compares
opportunities inside these compatible pillars and measured results decide the
future mix.

## First five candidate videos

- Hook: "GTA VI ja tem data, mas estes tres detalhes passaram despercebidos."
- Facts: official release on 19 November 2026; setting in Leonida; use only
  material and facts traceable to Rockstar.

  official-page references. Never reupload a trailer as the whole video.

### 2. Promotion or trap?

- Hook: "Um desconto grande pode esconder uma oferta ruim."
- Explain seller confidence, price history, shipping and return conditions.
- Use a fictional example until a real product URL is approved.

### 3. What an AI factory should not automate

- Hook: "Tres tarefas para a IA e duas decisoes que continuam humanas."
- Show research, script and editing versus identity, terms acceptance and ad
  spend.

### 4. Building from zero

- Hook: "Minha conta tem zero videos e eu vou testar uma fabrica de conteudo."
- Honest weekly progress, including failures and measured results.
- Never claim guaranteed follower or income results.

### 5. One trend, three evidence checks

- Hook: "Antes de copiar uma trend, eu verifico estas tres coisas."
- Check freshness, original source and visual potential in TikTok Creative
  Center and Google Trends.

## TikTok Shop status

The owner's screen requested 2,000 followers and recent posting. The current
official Creator Pilot policy states that affiliate creators under 2,000
followers can enter a limited 30-day pilot, but account eligibility and rollout
may still differ. After publishing original content, retry enrollment. If the
same blocker remains, open a TikTok Shop support ticket citing the current
Creator Pilot policy rather than trying to bypass the requirement.

## Publication gate

Every output remains a draft until Shin confirms the topic, factual sources,
visual rights and final caption. API posting is considered only after official
scopes are granted.
