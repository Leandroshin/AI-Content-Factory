# Channel architecture

Updated on 2026-07-11.

## Decision

Keep two distinct audience promises:

| Brand | Purpose | Primary channels |
|---|---|---|
| Achados Baratos BR | Deals, affiliate offers and smart-shopping education | Facebook, Instagram, Telegram and commerce profiles |
| Fase Nova Games | Gaming news, releases, explainers and long-form-to-Shorts | YouTube and TikTok |

`Achados Baratos BR` is a good commerce name, but a poor editorial gaming name.
A viewer who subscribes for bargains should not receive unrelated GTA or game
release coverage. Separating the brands improves expectation, topic clarity
and measurement without creating a separate technical factory.

## Gaming channel recommendation

- Display name: `Fase Nova Games`
- Preferred handle: `@fasenovagames`
- Positioning: verified gaming news, useful context and concise explanation
- Content: daily radar only when evidence exists; long explainers can generate
  separately reviewed Shorts

Handle availability must be confirmed inside each platform before account
creation. Do not add numbers or punctuation until the clean handle has been
checked.

Both brands use the same AI Content Factory, employees, provider controls and
HITL approval. They differ in editorial policy, visual identity, channel
credentials and performance metrics.
