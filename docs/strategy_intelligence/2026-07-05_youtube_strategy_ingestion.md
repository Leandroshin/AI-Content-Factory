# Youtube Strategy Ingestion - 2026-07-05

This note captures derived learning from the local transcript batch in `Conteudos do youtube/`.
Raw transcripts stay local and ignored by Git. The factory stores only operating knowledge,
checklists, signals, and safe handoff rules.

## What Employees Can Learn

- Product Research can learn metrics and decision rules: trend direction, revenue context, average ticket, commission, ranking/BSR, Buy Box, reviews, historical price, variation share, and saturation.
- Affiliate Deals can learn offer structure: product promise, discount/coupon, disclosure, channel fit, CTA, and approval status.
- Creative Review can learn whether a product image is already publishable or should be cleaned, replaced, or rebuilt.
- Script/Image/Video teams can learn repeatable formats such as POV product videos, proof-card thumbnails, short hooks, and benefit-first copy.
- Strategy Intelligence must extract patterns, not copy lessons, scripts, PDFs, courses, or creator assets.

## Patterns Captured

1. TikTok Shop POV AI video
   - Start from products already showing demand.
   - Prefer rising trend graphs over old top-revenue products that are declining.
   - Route product, image, script, and affiliate data into short-video production.

2. Amazon/Keepa product validation
   - Check ranking/BSR over multiple windows, historical price, reviews, Buy Box behavior, sellers, and variation share.
   - Do not assume a product is good just because the headline price looks attractive.

3. Affiliate post automation
   - Tools mentioned in transcripts include Divulga Ninja, Insta Ninja, AliExpress API, and social channels such as Telegram, WhatsApp, and Instagram.
   - The factory should use official APIs or owner-provided exports first, then add real adapters only when the workflow proves valuable.

4. Original infoproduct to checkout
   - AI can help plan an original product, cover, modules, deliverables, checkout, and launch assets.
   - Copied course content is not allowed; use external videos to infer structure and create original lessons/examples.

5. Thumbnail money-proof formula
   - Strong references use expressive face, platform logo, proof card, red action button, giant text, glow, and urgency.
   - Use the formula only. Do not reuse the creator's face, screenshot, fake proof, or third-party creative.

## Guardrails

- No guaranteed income claims. Fast-money claims must be treated as marketing until verified.
- Affiliate disclosure is mandatory before publishing.
- WhatsApp and Telegram automation require approval, rate limits, and compliance review.
- Product/course learning should produce original content and operational checklists, not rewritten source material.
- External tools can inspire adapters, but the project should not clone proprietary products.
