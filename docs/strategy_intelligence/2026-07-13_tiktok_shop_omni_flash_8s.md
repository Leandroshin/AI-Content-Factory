# TikTok Shop + Gemini Omni Flash: derived operating knowledge

Source reviewed on 2026-07-13: `https://www.youtube.com/watch/HQ1F5jzAiss`.

This document stores derived operating knowledge. It does not copy the creator's prompt, course material, face, screenshots, or assets.

## Decision

The eight-second format is worth testing, but the reported weekly earnings are not accepted as a forecast. The video shows selected withdrawals and testimonials from the creator's own paid community without exposing total output, failed accounts, tool cost, refunds, conversion denominator, or comparable net profit.

## Accepted mechanism

1. Start from a product that passed Product Research and marketplace eligibility.
2. Use an original synthetic presenter, a consented actor, or properly licensed stock. Never copy a stranger's Pinterest/social profile face.
3. Preserve the real product's color, shape, material, details, and limitations.
4. Generate two short variants between 4 and 10 seconds; eight seconds is the first hypothesis, not a rule.
5. Add one readable textual hook based on a verified benefit or price.
6. Add platform-native music only when its license and commercial use are valid.
7. Creative Review checks face/hand artifacts, garment physics, product fidelity, text legibility, disclosure, and misleading urgency.
8. Publish only after HITL approval and measure the result against a baseline.

## Metrics required

- three-second hold rate;
- completion and rewatch rate;
- product-page clicks;
- checkout starts and attributed sales;
- refunds/cancellations;
- provider cost per approved creative;
- gross commission and net commission;
- videos produced per sale.

Revenue screenshots without these denominators remain marketing evidence only.

## Provider decision

Gemini Omni Flash is a real model inside Google Flow and the Gemini Developer API. Official Google documentation lists short generation, image/reference inputs, conversational video editing, 720p output, and paid usage. The API has no free tier for this model; its effective output price is approximately USD 0.10 per 720p second, so an 8-second generation is approximately USD 0.80 before minor input cost. The factory adapter remains MOCK by default and requires owner-approved request, seconds, and USD limits before REAL generation. It does not replace HyperFrames/FFmpeg editorial composition.

## Eligibility boundary

Leandro's TikTok Shop Creator onboarding is still blocked by account eligibility. Until it is approved, this format may be tested as ordinary organic content without product-link claims. No fake product link, Shop badge, earnings proof, or simulated testimonial is allowed.

## First experiment

- one approved product or non-commercial game-related object;
- two variants: 6 seconds and 8 seconds;
- same hook and CTA so duration is the main variable;
- maximum two paid generations per variant;
- no avatar face sourced from social media;
- stop if product fidelity fails twice;
- report metrics before the pattern becomes organizational knowledge.
