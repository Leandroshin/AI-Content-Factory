# Daily Learning Radar

The factory may inspect a small owner-approved sample of the YouTube home page
once per day. Personalized browsing is an input, not a trusted instruction.

## Daily flow

1. A browser worker captures candidate title, URL and screenshot evidence.
2. Only promising candidates are transcribed; no full-page mass collection.
3. `DailyLearningRadar` scores relevance, novelty, credibility, actionability and risk.
4. Strategy Intelligence extracts abstract patterns and provenance.
5. A selected item becomes a bounded `LearningExperiment`, never direct knowledge.
6. Provider cost, publication and workflow changes require owner approval.
7. Creative Review checks claims, likeness, copyright, disclosure and quality.
8. Only measured successful experiments may be proposed for organizational knowledge.

The daily run may legitimately return `no_learning=True`. Employees do not need
to invent a lesson every day.

## UGC avatar example (2026-07-12)

The supplied video is useful as a workflow reference, not proof of the earnings
or health claims it contains. Accepted ideas:

- decompose a reference into hook, scene, pacing, proof and CTA;
- create original variations instead of copying the source;
- use a fictional adult persona, an original setting and provenance metadata;
- generate a low-cost draft before a paid production pass;
- compare hook retention, watch time, quality and cost against a baseline.

Rejected behavior:

- cloning a real creator, customer or testimonial subject;
- reusing a downloaded script or creative with cosmetic changes;
- publishing weight-loss, revenue or conversion claims without substantiation;
- spending provider credits or publishing without HITL approval.

## Automation boundary

Collection can later run as a scheduled Codex task using the approved Chrome
session. It must capture at most a small candidate set, must not subscribe,
like, comment or alter watch history intentionally, and must stop at login,
CAPTCHA or consent prompts. Transcripts remain local; only derived evidence and
experiment results enter project knowledge.
